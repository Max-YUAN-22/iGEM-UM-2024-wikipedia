What should you do if you have Cancer? In BC3000, you will be told “no cure”
In the Edwin Smith Papyrus, your symptoms will be recorded, but doctors have no idea about the causes

If you were in the Middle Ages, doctors will probably use bloodletting, or herbal remedies
Unfortunately, it won't ease your discomfort

If you were at the end of the nineteenth century, chemotherapy and radiation had emerged But because of the immaturity of technology and materials, You will suffer immensely from the side effects of your treatment.

Back in the modern era, advances in science and technology have driven the progress of cancer treatment. The research of targeted therapy and immunotherapy provides you with more treatment options However, you may not be able to afford the expensive treatment.

Now synthetic biology is coming into view... Now, we use synthetic biology methods to create a unique approach to killing cancer cells through a "controlled bidirectional calcium overload platform."

The pathway is named as “CaVenger X”

Calcium ions will flow into the cytoplasm from the outside of the cell, or be released from the calcium reservoir of the endoplasmic reticulum into the cytoplasm to play a role. Just like a sword
